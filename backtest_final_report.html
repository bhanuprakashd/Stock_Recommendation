7. Piotroski F-Score Integration

The Piotroski F-Score is a peer-reviewed academic model (Piotroski, 2000) that evaluates financial strength using 9 binary signals:

Profitability (4 points)

  • F1: ROA > 0
  • F2: Operating Cash Flow > 0
  • F3: ROA Improving
  • F4: Accruals Quality

Leverage (3 points)

  • F5: Debt Decreasing
  • F6: Liquidity Improving
  • F7: No Share Dilution

Efficiency (2 points)

  • F8: Gross Margin Improving
  • F9: Asset Turnover Improving

Score Interpretation

7-9Strong
5-6Moderate
0-4Weak
